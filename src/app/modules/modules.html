Key Notes (NgModule & Standalone)

  • Since Angular 16+, standalone components are common; NgModule is still present in many projects.
  • Modules are useful for feature boundaries, lazy loading, and shared structure.
  • Standalone defines dependencies at component level and reduces module boilerplate.

Pros

  • NgModule: clear separation and lazy loading structure.
  • Standalone: less boilerplate and direct dependency declaration.
