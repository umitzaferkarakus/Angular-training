Key Notes (Component Communication)

  • Use Input/Output for simple hierarchies; prefer services and RxJS for siblings/distant components.
  • Consider NGXS/NGRX etc. for global state.
  • Router params, query params, and shared services are also channels.

Pros

  • Multiple strategies fit different needs.

Cons

  • Wrong strategy increases coupling and complexity.
